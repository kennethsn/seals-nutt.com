About

Who We Are

Seals-Nutt is the technical consulting firm where industry meets academia. We tackle challenging projects for our clients to turn their visions into products just as dynamic as they are. Whether you are a small business looking for a fresh look on your website or a major research institution seeking development of a new application, we have the experience and resources to deliver.

Our Mission

We aim to empower ideas by providing the tools to make them successful.

Our Story

What do you get when an entreprenueral software engineer & graphic designer collaborates with a literary educator? Don't know? Well, Seals-Nutt was started to find the answer. Over the past few years, founders Kenneth Seals-Nutt and Vanessa Hunter have joined to work on fun, creative, and ultimately useful projects based in Technology, Design, and the Digital Humanities. Together, they have acquired a team of collaborators to take on domain-changing research topics, creative graphic visualizations, full-stack software development, and much more!

Science Stories

Software Development · Technical Research

Images capture human attention. When digital images circulate on the web, people often gather information from the visual content alone. There may be embedded metadata in an image, perhaps automatically generated by the digital camera the photographer used. People do not often embed descriptive metadata into digital images they share online. The standards for image application programming interfaces (APIs) that the International Image Interoperability Framework (IIF) created provide guidance for how to communicate descriptive, technical, and administrative metadata along with images on the web. We introduce a proof-of-concept application to demonstrate the power of publishing images and structured data to tell stories. We tell science stories in this application related to the lives of women involved in scientific research, many of these stories have not yet been told on the web, we aim to raise their profile.
